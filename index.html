About me

Bangya(Neutrino) Liu is a PhD candidate in ECE Department advised by Prof. Suman Banerjee, and a member of WiNGS Lab.

His research interests broadly lie in the fields of 3D vision, volumetric video streaming, cooperative perception, and wireless networking.

Bangya received his bachelor degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology with Electrical and Electronics Engineering as his major in 2019. He then obtained his master degree in ECE from University of Wisconsin-Madison in 2021.
